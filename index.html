Lecture 1 Content

Definition: A digital image is a representation of a two-dimensional image as a finite set of digital values, called pixels.

Image Processing: The process of manipulating digital images to improve or extract information.

Applications: Medical imaging, satellite imaging, industrial inspection, law enforcement, artistic effects.

Key Stages: Acquisition, Restoration, Morphological Processing, Segmentation, Representation & Description, Enhancement, Object Recognition, Compression.
